Our Story

Within the enchanting realm of Coorg, where rivers sing and the greens dance under a sunlit sky, sits the curious Amanvana Spa Resort. A wonder among the resorts in Coorg, this haven is tucked away in a tapestry of lush foliage, with the river painting shimmering strokes across the landscape.

Here, tranquility floats in the air like a gentle lullaby whispered by the leaves. The locale, abundant with verdant splendor and cozy river vistas, offers each family a nook of their own in the private bungalows. A quintessential puzzle piece in the mosaic of Coorg resorts, Amanvana serves as a splendid gateway for those keen to wander through the picture-perfect vistas of Coorg.

Settle into a hammock for a relaxing siesta or lose yourself in a book beside the River Kaveri. Our resort is brimming with cozy nooks for reading and unwinding, with an orchard of 300 varieties of fruit trees and home to 40 species of birds that call this place home.
